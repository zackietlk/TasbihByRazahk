How to Use
Counter
Tap anywhere on the main circle to count. The number in the centre updates with every tap.

Tap Set Target to choose a count goal and direction — count up towards a number or count down from it. The progress bar tracks your position in real time.

Tap Change Dhikr to switch what you're reciting. Tap Add Dhikr to build a quick sequence starting from the counter.

Reset Count saves the session to History and resets to zero.
Sequence
Build a session with multiple adhkar in order. Choose a preset like After Salah, or tap + Add Dhikr to build your own.

Set the count per dhikr using the number field next to Add Dhikr. Set Count Direction before starting.

Once running, the app moves to the next dhikr automatically when you finish. Use Restart, Skip, or End at any point.

Save your custom sequences as presets to reuse them.
Library
Browse adhkar by category — Common, Morning, Evening, After Prayer, and all 99 Names of Allah with transliteration and meaning.

Tap Count to go straight to the counter with that dhikr selected. Tap + Seq to add it to your sequence builder.
Goals
Set daily, weekly, or monthly targets for any dhikr. Tap + Add Goal, select a dhikr, choose your period and target count.

Goals update automatically as you count — in both Counter and Sequence mode. Progress is shown as a bar on each goal card.
History
Every completed session is logged here automatically — the dhikr, count, date, and whether it was a counter or sequence session.

Your lifetime total, number of sessions, and days active are shown at the top. All data stays privately on your device.
